Arcane is an animated television series set in the League of Legends universe. It is currently being developed and produced by Riot and animated by Fortiche Productions in Paris, France. Arcane dives into the delicate balance between the rich, Utopian city of Piltover Crest Piltover and the seedy, oppressed underground of Zaun Crest. Known across Runeterra as the "city of progress," many of the most brilliant minds call these cities home. But the creation of hextech, a way for any person to control magical energy, threatens that balance. The story follows the origins of two iconic League champions-and the power that will tear them apart. Like the game on which it is based, Arcane is aimed at a "16+" audience and will deal with some more adult subject matter.
